Welcome to Divine Doors Nepal

A Door To Divine

Zen Circle

Divine Doors Nepal is a serene meditation retreat located in Ilam, Nepal alongside the towering Himalayas and lush pine forests.

Founded in 2013 by Swami Prem Bikash, it offers a sanctuary for spiritual growth with daily meditations and monthly camps dedicated to Osho's teachings. The resort features the Buddha Hall for deep meditation and serves organic, locally grown food.

Just 2 hours from India-Nepal Mechi Border, it provides a peaceful environment for inner transformation and joy, supported by the profound energy of a Buddhafield.
